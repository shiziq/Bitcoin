Competitive Context

Rivaling M-Pesa

M-Pesa charges a percentage-based fee. Bato charges KSh 0.15 — flat. Same for KSh 500 or KSh 50,000.

M-Pesa — Percentage Model
Send KSh 500≈ KSh 15–50
Send KSh 5,000≈ KSh 50–150
Send KSh 50,000≈ KSh 500–1,500
Fee type: Percentage (1–3%)
Bato (KCB) — Flat Fee
Send KSh 500KSh 0.15
Send KSh 5,000KSh 0.15
Send KSh 50,000KSh 0.15
Fee type: FLAT — not percentage

A Kenyan business processing KSh 500K/month through M-Pesa pays ~KSh 5K–15K in fees. On Bato: ~KSh 500 in blockchain fees plus 2% spread on loads — commercially transformative.
